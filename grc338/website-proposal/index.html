About Us

Three college best friends. Two passions. One goal. Cafe Clementine was established in 2015 when Georgia, Brooke, and Sage decided to combine their passions for healthy living and good eats. With the goal to empower positive living, Cafe Clementine offers creative twists all your favorites. Our menu will get your taste buds dancing! We use locally sourced ingredients to make fresh and flavorful eats that will keep you energized throughout the day. Our goal is to make healthy eating fun and establish an environment where we can uplift others.
